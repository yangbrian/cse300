VoyageQuest RPG

VoyageQuest was a simple RPG game I made with my friend, Edmund Qiu as our high school senior project. It was created using the Java Slick2D library.

The goal of the project was to introduce us to game development concepts, collaborative coding practices, and software design patterns.

Many graphics were sourced from artists who put up their artwork for free usage by the game development community. Most character sprites were created by our friend, Wenyu Deng.
